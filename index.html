The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a future where technology, sustainability, and innovation drive progress. With a focus on digital transformation, green energy, and skilled workforce development, it aims to foster inclusive growth while addressing climate challenges, emerging as a leader in regional and global cooperation.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Three Zeros" vision aspires to create a world free from poverty, unemployment, and environmental harm. By promoting social business, self-employment, and green technologies, this vision seeks to build a sustainable and inclusive society where economic and environmental well-being go hand in hand.Dr. Yunus advocates for creating opportunities for self-employment, enabling individuals to become job creators rather than job seekers. By empowering people with tools and resources, the goal is to achieve zero unemployment.
